About Raghu Engineering College

College Code: RAGU | Participating in EAPCET Counselling

Raghu Engineering College stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education, guided by our motto "Knowledge is Power". We are committed to nurturing skilled engineers who are ready to meet the challenges of tomorrow's technological landscape.

Our Mission

To provide world-class engineering education and foster innovation, research, and entrepreneurship among our students.

Our Vision

To become a leading technical institution recognized globally for academic excellence and industry partnerships.

Advanced programs in communication systems, embedded systems, and signal processing

Mechanical Engineering

Comprehensive curriculum covering design, manufacturing, and thermal engineering

Civil Engineering

Focus on structural engineering, environmental engineering, and construction management
